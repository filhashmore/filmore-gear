## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of gear status across the entire FILMORE touring operation.

- **Total Inventory Items**: Count of all gear across FILMORE (band-owned), Joel, Reid, and Drew
- **Needs Maintenance**: Items requiring attention before next show
- **Consumables Needed**: Supplies that need restocking
- **Pending Acquisitions**: New gear requests awaiting management/artist approval
- **Acquire ASAP**: Urgent acquisition items

## Sections

### Inventory
Full gear catalog with status tracking (Good, Maintenance, Damaged, Unknown), show type classification (DRIVE, FLY, Storage), pack location tracking, and per-owner filtering.

### Consumables
Expendable supplies including tape, batteries, cables, and band supplies with stock status, pricing, and direct purchase links to Sweetwater and Amazon.

### Acquisitions
New gear research and approval workflow. PM submits with rent/own classification, pricing, and product links. Management and Artist can approve or deny.

### FLY Pack
Visual baggage assignment for fly dates. Shows checked bags and carry-ons per crew member (Joel, Drew, Reid, Tyler, Guest) with search-based highlighting.
